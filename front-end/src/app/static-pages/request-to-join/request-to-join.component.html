Our onboarding specialist will get in touch with you within 1-2 business days between 9am and 5pm EST at your preferred time window. Please expect the call to last approximately 15-20 minutes. Once verified, each member of your practice who is joining SalusConnect (including staff) must activate their account through the unique link they receive on their email and set a new password.
