We believe that finding and booking medical appointments should never be a challenge. Period.

That's why when you need a medical appointment, SalusConnect lets you book your appointments in 3 simple steps.

1
Search for a medical provider available at a place and day of your choosing.
2
Pick a time slot and request an appointment by either creating a new account or logging in to your existing account.
3
If the provider has enabled Instant Booking, you're all set. Otherwise, look out for the confirmation text or email.

At every step, SalusConnect is created
with Canadians in mind.

Whenver you need to see a medical provider, you can benefit from the work that SalusConnect has already done for you. That means:

No more endless phone calls to check for doctor availability.

Get instant appointment confirmations, where available.

Lose the guesswork while finding providers that are Accepting New Patients.

SalusConnect is free for you to use. You only pay your provider.

You get your own patient zone that keeps a record of all your past and upcoming appointments.
