Careers
We take this word very seriously. And that's why this section is called Careers and not Jobs.
We believe there are no shortcuts
When we started work on SimplyDoc, it quickly became apparent that we could do it either the easy way or the right way. The easy way would have allowed us to launch in sooner but would have required us to perform less research, spend less than needed time on product architecture and design and leave important features out which would be truly valuable to Canadians and the medical community. Any guesses which path we chose?
And values are non-negotiable
No matter what we do and where we go, we are driven by a set of core values that drive our actions and decisions.
Integrity. Commitment. Passion. Hardwork.
These are the values that we live by every day. This is what defines us. Today we are a startup. Tomorrow we will be a large company [:)]. We will do everything to ensure that these values don’t get diluted over time.
Consider a career with us
Scaling a startup takes grit, determination and a unique kind of never-say-die attitude. If you are looking to get involved in helping build a transformational platform that can potentially impact the lives of millions of Canadians every day, we’d love to chat.
We have openings in product, technology, marketing and sales. All positions are remote but we’d love it if you are in the Greater Toronto Area. That way we can hang out once in a while.
Send us an email with your resume at business@simplydoc.ca We look forward to speaking with you.
