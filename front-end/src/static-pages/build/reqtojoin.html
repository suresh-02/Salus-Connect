what to expect next
1. We will get in touch with you
Our onboarding specialist will get in touch with you within 1-2 business days between 9am and 5pm EST at your preferred time window. Please expect the call to last approximately 15-20 minutes. Amongst other things, our onboarding specialist will discuss the subsciption plan best suited to your practice’s needs. Don’t forget to consider the annual plans that offer up to 15% off compared to subscriptions billed monthly.
2. Get set up
Once verified, each member of your practice who is joining SimplyDoc (including staff) must activate their account through the unique link they receive on their email and set a new password.
3. The world is your oyester
That’s it! Start using the platform. We have built SimplyDoc with tender loving care. We’re confident that you will find it incredibly useful and productive.
